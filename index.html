Introduction to the IPC Laws

The Indian Penal Code (IPC) serves as the fundamental legal framework in India for establishing criminal liability related to specified offenses and setting exceptions to criminal liability for these offenses. It applies to all Indian citizens and individuals of Indian origin, regardless of location. The IPC is organized into 23 chapters and consists of 511 sections.
